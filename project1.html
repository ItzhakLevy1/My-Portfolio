A Drop Shipping E-commerce Chrome Extension Tool.

I have created this project in a form of a chrome extension to boost up performance in the Drop shipping
E-commerce world.
If you are selling on eBay or Shopify you can now enhance your performance to great extent.

This Drop shipping tool can copy the content from all of the text fields of your buyer's shipping information,
And paste it into the appropriate fields in your supplier's form,

Thus allowing you to fulfill your orders in about one tenth of the time and with virtually zero effort.
